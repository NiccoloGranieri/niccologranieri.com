Max in a Box

Max in a Box is a project that imposes spatial and temporal limitations on the construction of a Max patch. Confined in a 15 x 15 bang patch, this project will evolve in 60 second chunks limited by Instagram’s time limit on feed videos. The development of the patch will be improvised but also driven by the comments so make sure you engage! Subpatches and abstractions allowed.
